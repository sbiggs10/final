About Me

Howdy y'all! My name is Sadie Biggs. I am a 19 year old freshman in college an I am pursuing a degree in Digital Media. I was born in San Antonio into a military family. Throughout my childhood I lived in several different places, including Italy, Maryland, Illinois, and Texas. When I lived in Illinois, I discovered my love for gardening while visiting a friend's house. Ever since that experience, I have loved to garden. I created this website to help those who are considering raising their own plants to succeed in their efforts. I wanted to create a community of likeminded gardeners who live in southern Texas, so that we may encourage and give advice to one another. Happy Gardening! (:
