1. Understand the Climate and Growing Seasons

8. Be Prepared for Extreme Weather

South Texas is known for its extremes, including heat waves, storms, and occasional freezes. Being prepared can help protect your garden from sudden weather changes.

Shade Cloth: In the heat of summer, use shade cloth to protect plants from scorching sun.
