Annuals are plants that must be planted every year. They do not survive the winter in most cases. They are more likely to be able to survive the winter in warmer climates. It is best practice to plant annuals in the spring so that they can be enjoyed until the first frost.

Perennials are plants that come back every year or stay year round. These types of plants are great for those who want to have low maintenance plants. Although these plants tend to come back every year, if it is extra cold durng the winter they may not survive.

Texas weather is very unpredictable. It does tend to be very hot in the summer with quite a few days over 100 degrees Fahrenheit. Fall, winter, and spring are much more unpredictable. Some days it will feel like a warm summer day, while others will be frigid.
